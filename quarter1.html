Quarter 1

We have chosen to create Vlogs based on our daily lives as students. These vlogs will not only rely on our day-to-day activities—but will involve learning and reflection in life. The videos will showcase our daily routines and any particular activities that will entertain viewers. Based on survey results, our target audience has chosen the following topics for our videos: student life routine, hobbies, fun challenges, and travelling. We will record our vlogs based on our skill for these categories.

Our target audience are students ranging from 13 to 18 years old. Our goal is to make relatable and relaxing content for students who want a break from their busy school lives. While our target audience will mainly be students, as a whole, our target audience are people who are looking for relaxing content that will entertain them and leave them with a message to reflect on.
