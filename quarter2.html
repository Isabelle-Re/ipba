Quarter 2

YouTube Channel Creation

Our group chose the content type vlogging–with our topic being centered around our lives as students by including daily routines and challenges. In planning for our Youtube channel’s name, we decided to be inspired from school/class elements. This inclusion of school elements in the name would clearly express our channel’s theme and branding to our viewers. Before we chose our logo, we had a lot of good options we could choose from. But, we ultimately chose the logo that best suited our tagline and channel name/handle. And that’s how we came to a conclusion and the decision of just making or visualizing a diary. Because a diary holds the memories and the journey we would be going through.

Our tagline is “Keeping school memories alive”. We selected this from the options: The best chapters in our book of life, Your daily dose of school life, Because every school day has a story/Because every school day deserves a story, and Keeping school memories alive. It shows that our channel aims to capture and share the fun, challenges and remembering sharing our school experiences. We want the viewers to feel part of the everyday moments of school life, whether that's in something fun and challenging or an important lesson or friendship along the way. We want to remind people through our vlogs that school is not only about studying, it is living and making memories that form our personality.
